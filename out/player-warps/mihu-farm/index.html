Guide

Any tool you took from the Player Warp must be returned
Unless you have serious reasons for keeping it
Any donations are welcome; you will be placed on a wall of fame
You will need at least Ranger rank to be verified
Don't break anything except the crops and always replant them
Make sure to use a hoe/item that auto-replants; Demeters Touch is available in the basement.
